# SmartStay Deployment Guide

Deploying your newly created Monorepo consists of two parts: deploying the **Frontend** (Next.js) to Vercel, and deploying the **Backend** (Node.js/Express) to Render.

## 1. Deploy the Backend to Render (Free)

1. Go to [Render.com](https://render.com/) and create an account.
2. In the Render Dashboard, click **New +** -> **Web Service**.
3. Under "Connect a repository", search for and select your GitHub repository: `vansh-pixel/SmartStay-frontend`.
4. Configure the Web Service:
   - **Name**: `smartstay-backend`
   - **Root Directory**: `SmartStay-backend` (⚠️ **CRITICAL: Must match exactly**)
   - **Environment**: `Node`
   - **Region**: Choose the one closest to your users.
   - **Branch**: `main`
   - **Build Command**: `npm install`
   - **Start Command**: `npm start`
   - **Instance Type**: Free
5. Scroll down and click **Advanced** -> **Add Environment Variable**. Add all your backend variables from your `.env`:
   - `MONGO_URI` = *(Copy from your SmartStay-backend/.env file)*
   - `GROQ_API_KEY` = *(Copy from your SmartStay-backend/.env file)*
   - `JWT_SECRET` = *(Copy from your SmartStay-backend/.env file)*
   - `STRIPE_SECRET_KEY` = *(Copy from your SmartStay-backend/.env file)*
   - `EMAIL_USER` = *(Copy from your SmartStay-backend/.env file)*
   - `EMAIL_PASS` = *(Copy from your SmartStay-backend/.env file)*
   - `GOOGLE_CLIENT_ID` = *(Copy from your SmartStay-backend/.env file)*
   - `CLIENT_URL` = We will update this later once the frontend is deployed. For now, you can leave it out or set it to `https://smartstay.vercel.app`.
6. Click **Create Web Service** at the bottom.
7. Wait a few minutes for the build to finish. Once done, you will see a URL generated by Render (e.g., `https://smartstay-backend-abcd.onrender.com`). **Copy this URL**.

---

## 2. Deploy the Frontend to Vercel (Free)

1. Go to [Vercel.com](https://vercel.com/) and create an account using your GitHub.
2. Click **Add New** -> **Project**.
3. Import the `vansh-pixel/SmartStay-frontend` repository.
4. Configure the Project:
   - **Project Name**: `smartstay`
   - **Framework Preset**: `Next.js`
   - **Root Directory**: Click "Edit" and choose `SmartStay-frontend` (⚠️ **CRITICAL**)
5. Open the **Environment Variables** tab and add:
   - `NEXT_PUBLIC_GOOGLE_CLIENT_ID` = `846798455763-75uf3j4e4vpf2evln1sgrmbct44v1en0.apps.googleusercontent.com`
   - `NEXT_PUBLIC_API_URL` = `<YOUR_RENDER_BACKEND_URL>/api` *(Replace `<YOUR_RENDER_BACKEND_URL>` with the URL you copied from Render, e.g., `https://smartstay-backend-abcd.onrender.com/api`)*
6. Click **Deploy**.
7. Once finished, Vercel will give you a live URL for your website (e.g., `https://smartstay.vercel.app`). **Copy this URL**.

---

## 3. Final Step: Configure Server CORS

Now that you have your Vercel frontend URL, go back to your **Render Backend Dashboard**:
1. Open the **Environment** tab.
2. Add or update the variable `CLIENT_URL` to be your Vercel URL (e.g., `https://smartstay.vercel.app` - no trailing slash at the end).
3. Click **Save Changes**. Render will automatically restart your backend.

Your website is now **fully live on the internet!** 🚀
